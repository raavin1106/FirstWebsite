TERMINAL 1

The Social Tree

Share your photo and video memories of Changi instantly on this nine-metre interactive tree.

GST Refund

Claim a refund of the GST paid at participating retailers with our eTRS (Electronic Tourist Refund Scheme) machines.

Kinetic Rain

Stand mesmerised by the dance of 1,216 polished copper raindrops. The art depicts the joy of travelling.

Viewing Mall

The viewing mall @ T1, T2 and T3 allow aviation enthusiasts and plane spotters to observe Changi Airport's ground operations on the tarmac.

Catcus Garden

Visit the rooftop garden with over 100 cacti and arid plants from Africa and the Americas. Chill out the bar with beverages before your flight.

TERMINAL 2

Enchanted Garden

Get enthralled by the and decorative Enchanted Garden as it "magically" comes to life with sight and sound.

Entertainment Deck

From Xbox 360 and Kinect stations to a movie theatre, its your one-stop multimedia entertainment destination.

Clinics and pharmacies

Unwell? Visit the Raffles medical group clinics @ T2 and all other terminals.

Free-to-use-rest areas

Tired? Come and get a shut-eye at world's best airport for sleeping.

TERMINAL 3

Butterfly Garden

The first butterfly garden is home to over 1,000 butterfly species. Come and learn about their life cycle at the educational corners and individual enclosures.

Crystal Garden

The Crystal Garden comprises of 1,735 hand blown Bohemian glass components. Embracing the philosophy that soothes, the Crystal Garden gently integrates nature into the Central Piazza, presenting travellers with an interesting juxtaposition of poetic cakm amidst a burstt of colour and activity.

TERMINAL 4

Heritage Zone

Inspired by Peranakan shophouses nestled around Singapore, this zone houses famous traditional brands like Bengawan Solo, Curry Times etc. with heritage themed facades, interior design and furnishings to invoke a sense of nostalgia.

Immersive Wall

Located at the Centralised Security area, LED display entertains passengers waiting at security screen queues.

Les Oiseaux

"Les Oiseaux (The Birds)" is a beautiful handcrafted stainless steel wired sculpture of three birds, by French artist, Cedric Le Borgne.

Peranakan Gallery

Learn more about the heritage and culture of the Singapore Peranakan community at the Peranakan Gallery, with embroided Sarong Kebaya, Peranakan porcelain 'nyonyaware' and other historical exhibits up on display.

*NOTE: The above listed venues are just examples from each terminal. There are many other things to explore at Singapore Changi. For further information visit attractions & services
